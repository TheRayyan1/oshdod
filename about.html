About Oshdod Holdings

Founded with a vision to bridge markets and create opportunities, Oshdod Holdings has established itself as a premier business development and investment firm focused on connecting Egyptian and Saudi markets.

Our deep understanding of both markets' unique characteristics, regulatory environments, and business cultures allows us to identify valuable opportunities and facilitate successful cross-border ventures.

We are committed to fostering economic growth, promoting innovation, and creating sustainable value for all our stakeholders through strategic partnerships and innovative business solutions.

Our Mission

To empower businesses by creating bridges between Egyptian and Saudi markets, fostering economic growth and sustainable partnerships.

Market Expertise

We leverage our deep understanding of both Egyptian and Saudi markets to identify opportunities and navigate challenges.

Strategic Networking

We connect businesses with the right partners, investors, and resources to facilitate successful ventures.
